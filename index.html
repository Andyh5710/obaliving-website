Living in Orange Beach

Orange Beach offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, recreational activities, and small-town charm with big-city amenities.

32 Miles of Pristine Beaches

Enjoy sugar-white sand beaches along the Gulf of Mexico with crystal clear waters.

World-Class Fishing

Known as the "Red Snapper Capital of the World" with deep sea, bay, and pier fishing options.

Award-Winning Dining

Fresh seafood restaurants, waterfront dining, and local favorites throughout the area.

Championship Golf

Several top-rated golf courses designed by legends like Arnold Palmer and Jerry Pate.
